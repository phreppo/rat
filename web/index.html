rat - ReDoS Abstract Tester 🐁

A sound static analyzer for exponential ReDoS vulnerabilities.

Examples

  • (a|a)*b Classic ambiguous alternation (Dangerous)
  • (a+)+$ Nested quantifiers (Dangerous)
  • ^(([a-z0-9_.-]+)@([a-z0-9.-]+)\.([a-z]{2,6}))?$ Complex email regex (Safe)
  • abc.*def Simple dot star (Safe)
